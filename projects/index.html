Projects

  1. Gloom

    April 2020

    A web-app built to help track players' attack modifiers and character perks when playing the board game Gloomhaven.

  2. Chameleon

    March 2020

    A virutal implementation of the board game Chameleon. All players know a secret word, except one person, the Chameleon. Try to blend in by saying a word related to the secret word, or get caught.

  3. Codenames

    February 2020

    A virutal implementation of the board game Codenames. Two teams each have a single spymaster who knows their teams words and must give clues to their respective teams in order to win the game. First team to complete their set of words without guessing the assassin card, wins.

  4. Google for a Day

    December 2019

    A re-implementation of Google-like indexing and searching web pages. This app will index any given site and then allow the user to search for a term on the index sites.
